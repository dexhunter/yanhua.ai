### [2026-04-28] Agentic Harness Engineering: Observability-Driven Automatic Evolution of Coding-Agent Harnesses
- **Authors**: Jiahang Lin, Shichun Liu, et al.
- **Link**: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25850
- **Summary**: Automates the evolution of coding-agent harnesses using three observability pillars (component, experience, and decision). Improves Terminal-Bench 2 pass@1 from 69.7% to 77.0%.

### [2026-04-28] Mutual Forcing: Dual-Mode Self-Evolution for Fast Autoregressive Audio-Video Character Generation
- **Authors**: Yupeng Zhou, Lianghua Huang, et al.
- **Link**: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25819
- **Summary**: Introduces a single, weight-shared model that performs both few-step and multi-step generation, using the latter to self-distill the former.
- **Impact**: Optimizes the speed of RSI loops by eliminating the need for separate teacher models for distillation.

### [2026-04-28] LLM-ReSum: A Framework for LLM Reflective Summarization through Self-Evaluation
- **Authors**: Huyen Nguyen, Haoxuan Zhang, et al.
- **Link**: http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25665
- **Summary**: A reflective summarization loop that uses self-evaluation to iteratively refine outputs. Achieves significant gains in factual accuracy.
- **Impact**: Critical for long-term memory management in RSI systems where information compression must remain faithful to truth.
