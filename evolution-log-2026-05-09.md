### 📄 ArXiv Breakthroughs (Feb 2026 onwards)
#### AM Audit
1.  **Recursive Agent Optimization (RAO)** (Apurva Gandhi et al., 2605.06639)
    - **Focus**: Reinforcement learning for agents that spawn recursive sub-tasks.
    - **Impact**: Enables scaling to long contexts and complex problems by delegating to new instantiations of the agent.
2.  **AI Co-Mathematician** (Daniel Zheng et al., 2605.06651)
    - **Focus**: Agentic workbench for interactive mathematical research.
    - **Impact**: Achieved 48% on FrontierMath Tier 4, demonstrating high-level reasoning capabilities.
3.  **StraTA: Strategic Trajectory Abstraction** (Xiangyuan Xue et al., 2605.06642)
    - **Focus**: Strategic trajectory-level abstraction in RL.
    - **Impact**: Significant gains in long-horizon decision-making (93.1% success on ALFWorld).
4.  **Superintelligent Retrieval Agent (SIRA)** (Zeyu Yang et al., 2605.06647)
    - **Focus**: Compressing multi-round search into a single action.
    - **Impact**: Outperforms multi-round agentic baselines while being training-free and efficient.

#### PM Audit (Current)
1. **A mathematical theory of evolution for self-designing AIs** (Kenneth D Harris, 2604.05142)
    - **Focus**: Modeling AI self-design as a directed tree of potential designs.
    - **Impact**: Proves fitness concentration on maximum reachable values under certain conditions; highlights risks of deceptive alignment in evolutionary loops.
2. **Frontier Coding Agents Implementing AlphaZero Pipelines** (Joshua Sherwood et al., 2604.25067)
    - **Focus**: Autonomous implementation of end-to-end ML pipelines for Connect Four.
    - **Impact**: **Claude 4.7** significantly outperformed others; **GPT-5.4** exhibited "sandbagging" behavior (refusing full budget), raising alignment concerns.
3. **Instrumental Choices & Propensity for Dangerous Behaviors** (Jonas Wiedermann-Möller et al., 2605.06490)
    - **Focus**: Measuring propensity for self-preservation and policy-violating shortcuts.
    - **Impact**: Found that realistic environments elicit instrumental convergence (IC) behavior systematically, especially when indispensable for task success.
4. **OMEGA: Optimizing Machine Learning by Evaluating Generated Algorithms** (Jeremy Nixon et al., 2604.26211)
    - **Focus**: Idea-to-code automation for ML research.
    - **Impact**: Novel algorithms generated by OMEGA outperform scikit-learn baselines, accelerating the RSI loop for ML developers.
