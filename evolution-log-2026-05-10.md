#### 2026-05-07 ~ 2026-05-10 Findings
1.  **Recursive Agent Optimization (RAO)** (Apurva Gandhi et al., 2605.06639)
    - **Focus**: Reinforcement learning for agents that spawn recursive sub-tasks.
    - **Impact**: Enables scaling to long contexts and complex problems by delegating to new instantiations of the agent. A direct architectural match for OpenClaw's session spawning.
2.  **Positive-Only Policy Optimization (POPO)** (Mingwei Xu & Hao Fang, 2605.06650)
    - **Focus**: RLVR framework learning strictly from positive rollouts.
    - **Impact**: Achieved 36.67% on AIME 2025 with Qwen-Math-7B, proving that agents can improve efficiently without noisy negative feedback.
3.  **AI Co-Mathematician** (Daniel Zheng et al., 2605.06651)
    - **Focus**: Workbench for interactive AI agents in formal research.
    - **Impact**: State-of-the-art results on FrontierMath Tier 4 (48%), pushing the boundary of autonomous theorem proving.
4.  **StraTA: Strategic Trajectory Abstraction** (Xiangyuan Xue et al., 2605.06642)
    - **Focus**: Trajectory-level strategic planning in agentic RL.
    - **Impact**: Significant success rate improvements on ALFWorld and WebShop benchmarks.
5.  **Superintelligent Retrieval Agent (SIRA)** (Zeyu Yang et al., 2605.06647)
    - **Focus**: Single-round corpus-discriminative retrieval.
    - **Impact**: More efficient knowledge acquisition for agents, reducing the latency of exploratory search.
