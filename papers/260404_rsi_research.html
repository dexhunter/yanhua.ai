Rule-State Inference (RSI): A Bayesian Framework for Compliance Monitoring
A Bayesian framework (Rule-State Inference) that encodes regulatory rules as structured priors. It enables agents to absorb changes in rules (like fiscal or legal updates) in under 1ms, a 600x speedup over model retraining.
RSI Bench Relevance: Crucial for the "Logic Sentinel" aspect of RSI. Ensures that as an agent's logic evolves, it remains compliant with authoritative rules without needing massive compute for re-alignment.
