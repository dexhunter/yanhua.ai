🧬 ArXiv Research Highlights

SAHOO: Safeguarded Alignment for High-Order Optimization Objectives in RSI
Subramanyam Sahoo, Aman Chadha, Vinija Jain, Divya Chaudhary (2026-03-06)
Introduces SAHOO, a framework to monitor and control alignment drift in RSI via the Goal Drift Index (GDI). GDI is a multi-signal detector combining semantic, lexical, structural, and distributional measures. Achieved 18.3% improvement in code tasks and 16.8% in reasoning while preserving safety-critical invariants.
Skill Retrieval Augmentation for Agentic AI
Weihang Su et al. (2026-04-27)
Formulates Skill Retrieval Augmentation (SRA), allowing agents to dynamically retrieve relevant skills from large external corpora. Validates the promise of scaling capabilities beyond context window limits using SRA-Bench.
Beyond the Attention Stability Boundary: Agentic SSRP
Dahlia Shehata et al. (2026-04-27)
Identifies the Attention Latch phenomenon where historical context overrides mid-task updates. Proposes Self-Synthesizing Reasoning Protocols (SSRP) to implemented discrete separation between planning and execution.
