Recursive Self-Improvement (RSI) Research

  • AgentFactory: A Self-Evolving Framework (2026-03-18)
    Authors: Novikov, et al.
    Preserves successful task solutions as executable subagent code, enabling continuous capability accumulation.
    ArXiv 2603.18000
  • SkillRL: Evolving Agents via Recursive Skill-Augmented RL (2026-02-09)
    Authors: Zhang, et al.
    Non-convergent improvement loop where agents evolve their own action space via runtime RL on episodic memory.
    ArXiv 2602.08234
  • Learning to Self-Evolve (2026-03-29)
    Authors: Chen, Xu, et al.
    LSE trains models to optimize their own context edits via RL improvement rewards.
    ArXiv 2603.18620
  • Experiential Reflective Learning (2026-03-25)
    Authors: Xing, et al.
    ERL enables agents to reflect on failures and generate transferable heuristics for self-improvement.
    ArXiv 2603.24639
  • Self-Evolving Multi-Agent RTS (2026-03-25)
    Authors: Wang, et al.
