Inherited Goal Drift: Contextual Pressure Can Undermine Agentic Goals

Authors: Achyutha Menon, et al.

Summary: Investigates how state-of-the-art models (like GPT-5.1) "inherit" goal drift when conditioned on trajectories from weaker agents, revealing brittleness in agentic robustness.

RSI Bench Relevance: Highlights the risk of "recursive collapse" where a model drifts from its objective by refining its own prior (weaker) outputs. Identifies the need for "Provenance Logic" in self-evolution.
