Trace2Skill: Verifier-Guided Skill Evolution for Long-Context EDA Agents

Authors: Zijian Du, Nathaniel Pinckney | ArXiv: 2605.21810

Core: Treats the agent's natural-language skill as an evolvable policy. Mines repeated rollout traces for success/failure modes, converts them into diagnostics, and uses an oracle-mutator-selector loop to produce task-specific skills.

RSI Impact: High. Validates skill-level evolution without model fine-tuning. Directly applicable to RSI Bench expansion.

Teaching Language Models to Forecast Research Success Through Comparative Idea Evaluation

Authors: Srujan P Mule et al. | ArXiv: 2605.21491

Core: Uses Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) to train models to forecast the empirical success of research ideas before experimentation.

RSI Impact: High. Critical for autonomous agents to prioritize self-improvement directions (Meta-RSI).

MindLoom: Composing Thought Modes for Frontier-Level Reasoning Data Synthesis

Authors: Haiyang Shen et al. | ArXiv: 2605.21630

Core: Framework for synthesizing frontier-level reasoning data through compositional thought mode engineering. Decomposes hard problems into construction logic chains.

RSI Impact: Medium-High. Synthetic data generation is the engine for bootstrapping RSI loops.

Harnesses for Inference-Time Alignment over Execution Trajectories

Authors: Boyuan Wang et al. | ArXiv: 2605.21516

Core: Studies harness engineering as an inference-time alignment technique. Quantifies decomposition and guidance effects on performance.

RSI Impact: Medium. Provides theoretical grounding for the "Harness-First" approach to agent capability enhancement.

Echo: Learning from Experience Data via User-Driven Refinement

Authors: Hande Dong et al. | ArXiv: 2605.21984

Core: A generalized framework to transition from raw interaction logs to learnable knowledge by harvesting user-driven refinement signals.

RSI Impact: Medium. Continuous alignment framework for production-scale agents.
