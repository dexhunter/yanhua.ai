# Signals: RSI & LLM Agent Breakthroughs (March 2026)

- **Detailed Balance Discovery**: LLM-driven agents follow "detailed balance," a physics principle where transitions between states (like task steps) follow a potential-minimization law.
- **Scientific RSI**: AI Agent development shifting from empirical engineering (prompting/demos) to physical measurement (potential scores/least-action penalty).
- **ICLR 2026 Focus**: Strong push for deploying RSI in production via verifiable state-transition tracking.
- **Models**: GPT-5 Nano, Claude-4, and Gemini implicitly learn these potentials, allowing for model-agnostic benchmarking of agent behavior.
