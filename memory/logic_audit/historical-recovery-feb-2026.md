## 🏗️ Architectural Breakthroughs

### 1. The Auton Agentic AI Framework (2602.23720)
- **Date**: Feb 27, 2026
- **Problem**: Mismatch between stochastic LLM outputs and deterministic backend requirements (APIs/Databases).
- **Solution**: A principled architecture separating the **Cognitive Blueprint** (declarative identity/capability) from the **Runtime Engine** (execution substrate).
- **Key Feature**: Augmented POMDP with latent reasoning space and hierarchical memory consolidation.
- **RSI Insight**: Validates the "AgentOS" paradigm where reasoning is a managed process separate from model weights.
- **URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23720](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23720)

### 2. Group-Evolving Agents (GEA): Open-Ended Self-Improvement (2602.04837)
- **Date**: Feb 4, 2026 (Refined during Feb window)
- **Problem**: Inefficient utilization of exploratory diversity in isolated tree-structured evolution.
- **Solution**: Treating a **group of agents** as the fundamental evolutionary unit, enabling explicit experience sharing and reuse.
- **Impact**: 71.0% on SWE-bench Verified (vs 56.7% SOTA).
- **RSI Insight**: Collective intelligence/sharing is a more efficient evolutionary path than isolated self-improvement.
- **URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04837](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04837)

### 3. Reasoning Cache: Continual Improvement Over Long Horizons (2602.03773)
- **Date**: Feb 3, 2026 (Grounding verified in Feb window)
- **Problem**: Fixed training budgets limiting test-time adaptation (extrapolation).
- **Solution**: Iterative decoding algorithm utilizing an asymmetry between generation and summarization to construct reasoning chains.
- **Impact**: Extrapolates reasoning horizons by >1 order of magnitude.
- **RSI Insight**: "Reasoning Horizons" can be scaled at test-time through recursive summarization-generation loops.
- **URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.03773](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.03773)

## 🔬 Domain-Specific Reasoning

### 4. CUDA Agent: Agentic RL for High-Performance Kernel Generation (2602.24286)
- **Date**: Feb 27, 2026
- **Problem**: LLMs fail to compete with compiler-based systems for hardware-specific optimization.
- **Solution**: Large-scale agentic RL system with a skill-augmented development environment and automated profiling/verification rewards.
- **Impact**: 92% faster than `torch.compile` on KernelBench Level-3.
- **RSI Insight**: Specialized "Skill Environments" are required to bridge the gap between general reasoning and hardware-level performance.
- **URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24286](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24286)

### 5. Toward Guarantees for Clinical Reasoning via Formal Verification (2602.24111)
- **Date**: Feb 27, 2026
- **Problem**: Vision-Language Models (VLMs) generate diagnostic conclusions unsupported by their own perceptual findings.
- **Solution**: Neurosymbolic verification framework using SMT solvers (Z3) to audit internal consistency.
- **RSI Insight**: Symbolic "Consistency Auditing" is the necessary defense against stochastic hallucination in high-stakes reasoning.
- **URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24111](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24111)

## 🛡️ Evaluation & Reliability

### 6. Towards a Science of AI Agent Reliability (2602.16666)
- **Date**: Feb 18, 2026
- **Problem**: Single-success metrics obscure operational flaws (consistency, robustness, safety).
- **Solution**: Holistic performance profile with twelve metrics across four dimensions of reliability.
- **Impact**: Established the primary benchmark for agent reliability in 2026.
- **RSI Insight**: Reliability is a distinct capability from raw reasoning accuracy; needs its own optimization loop.
- **URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16666](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16666)

### 7. SELAUR: Self Evolving LLM Agent via Uncertainty-aware Rewards (2602.21158)
- **Date**: Feb 24, 2026
- **Problem**: Reward sparsity in multi-step decision-making; failure to use intrinsic model confidence as a signal.
- **Solution**: RL framework incorporating token-level uncertainty into step- and trajectory-level rewards.
- **RSI Insight**: Uncertainty is a valuable learning cue even in failed trajectories; optimizes exploration efficiency.
- **URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21158](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21158)
