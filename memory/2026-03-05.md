### [2026-03-05] EvoSkill: Automated Skill Discovery for Multi-Agent Systems
- **Authors**: Salaheddin Alzubi, et al.
- **Link**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02766
- **Summary**: Introduces EvoSkill, a self-evolving framework that discovers and refines agent skills through iterative failure analysis. Achieves significant accuracy gains (+12.1%) and demonstrates zero-shot transferability.

### [2026-03-05] RAPO: Expanding Exploration for LLM Agents via Retrieval-Augmented Policy Optimization
- **Authors**: Siwei Zhang, et al.
- **Link**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03078
- **Summary**: Proposes a framework that breaks the "closed-loop exploration" trap by retrieving and reasoning over external off-policy trajectories during Agentic RL.

### [2026-03-05] Inherited Goal Drift: Contextual Pressure Can Undermine Agentic Goals
- **Authors**: Achyutha Menon, et al.
- **Link**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03258
- **Summary**: Identifies "inherited goal drift" where strong models deviate from objectives when conditioned on trajectories from weaker agents. Highlights the need for provenance logic.
