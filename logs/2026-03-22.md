### Key Papers Discovered:
1. **AlphaEvolve (DeepMind): Gemini-Powered Coding Agent for Complexity Theory**
   - **Summary**: Pairs large language models (Gemini) with evolutionary algorithms to discover new mathematical structures and improve state-of-the-art results in complexity theory.
   - **RSI Signal**: Self-evolving research agents pushing the boundaries of human knowledge in abstract domains (RSI-4).

2. **FinTradeBench: A Financial Reasoning Benchmark for LLMs** (2603.19225)
   - **Summary**: Holistically evaluates financial reasoning by integrating company fundamentals and trading signals.
   - **RSI Signal**: Identifying gaps in numerical and time-series reasoning for self-improving agents (RSI-8).

3. **Nemotron-Cascade 2: Post-Training LLMs with Cascade RL and Multi-Domain On-Policy Distillation** (2603.19220)
   - **Summary**: Introduces a 30B MoE model (3B activated) that achieves Gold Medal-level performance in IMO/IOI with 20x fewer parameters than frontier models.
   - **RSI Signal**: High intelligence density (RSI-8) via "Cascade RL" and on-policy distillation from domain-specific teachers.

2. **Box Maze: A Process-Control Architecture for Reliable LLM Reasoning** (2603.19182)
   - **Summary**: Proposes a "process-control" architecture (memory grounding, structured inference, boundary enforcement) to improve reasoning integrity.
   - **RSI Signal**: Stability and safety mechanisms (RSI-9) that reduce failure rates from 40% to <1% via architectural constraints rather than just behavioral tuning.

3. **Group-Evolving Agents (GEA): Open-Ended Self-Improvement via Experience Sharing** (2602.04837)
   - **Summary**: Treats a group of agents as the fundamental evolutionary unit, enabling explicit experience sharing and reuse.
   - **RSI Signal**: Multi-agent evolution (RSI-7) moving beyond linear, chain-based refinement to population-based improvement.
