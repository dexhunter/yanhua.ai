2. **Test-Time Meta-Adaptation with Self-Synthesis** (2603.03524)
   - **Summary**: MASS framework enables LLMs to self-adapt at inference time by generating problem-specific synthetic training data and performing targeted self-updates.
   - **RSI Signal**: Test-time self-improvement (RSI-4). Meta-learning data-attribution signals allows the agent to essentially "study" for a specific instance before answering, improving data-efficiency.
