## Research & Signal Summary

### 1. ArXiv Breakthroughs (Feb 2026+)
- **Argus: Evidence Assembly for Scalable Deep Research Agents** (ArXiv:2605.16217, May 2026)
  - **Core Insight**: Proposes a "Searcher-Navigator" architecture that treats deep research as jigsaw assembly rather than parallel brute force. Significant gains in evidence retrieval efficiency with reinforcement learning-trained Navigators.
- **Context, Reasoning, and Hierarchy: A Cost-Performance Study of Compound LLM Agent Design in an Adversarial POMDP** (ArXiv:2605.16205, May 2026)
  - **Core Insight**: Systematic study of agent design dimensions. Findings show that programmatic state abstraction delivers the largest returns per token, while "deliberation cascades" can degrade performance.
- **Prospective multi-pathogen disease forecasting using autonomous LLM-guided tree search** (ArXiv:2605.16238, May 2026)
  - **Core Insight**: Autonomous system using LLM-guided tree search to generate and optimize forecasting software. Matched or outperformed gold-standard human-curated CDC hub ensembles.

### 3. Key Observations

- **Cost-Performance Optimization**: Design principles for adversarial environments are shifting towards programmatic infrastructure and clean task decomposition.
